Our Story
There are very few places in the world where nature, culture, and opportunity come together as
effortlessly as Sylhet.
Blessed with emerald tea gardens, crystal-clear rivers, rolling hills, and the enchanting haors,
Sylhet has long been known as Bangladesh’s premier tourism destination. It is the cherished
homecoming of millions of Non-Resident Bangladeshis (NRBs), a thriving center of commerce
powered by natural gas and industry, and a region celebrated for its warmth, hospitality, and
timeless traditions.
From this remarkable landscape, Sylhet Boat Club Limited was born.

Why Sylhet Boat Club?
Imagine beginning your morning with the sunrise reflecting over the Sari Gowain River.
Spend the day kayaking through calm waters, enjoying riverside sports, or hosting an executive
meeting overlooking the river.
In the afternoon, embark on a boat journey to Ratargul Swamp Forest, Lalakhal, Shada Pathor,
or Jaflong before returning for an elegant dinner by the water.
As evening falls, families gather on the green lawns while friends reconnect over coffee, and
business relationships evolve into lasting partnerships.
This would be the experience Sylhet Boat Club promises, where every moment celebrates
nature, elegance, and community.
We believe within short span of time the Club will be most preferred club in Sylhet.
